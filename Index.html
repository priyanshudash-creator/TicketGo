Featured Trains

The Featured Trains section highlights some of the most popular and frequently booked trains on your TicketGo website. It helps users quickly discover well-known train services without searching manually.

  • Vande Bharat Express - A modern semi-high-speed train offering comfortable seating, onboard catering, and faster travel between major cities.
  • Rajdhani Express - A premium long-distance train connecting New Delhi with state capitals, known for its speed and complimentary meals.
  • Duronto Express - A long-distance train with limited stops, designed for faster travel between major cities.
  • Shatabdi Express - A premium daytime train with air-conditioned coaches, ideal for short to medium-distance journeys.
  • Garib Rath Express - An affordable air-conditioned train that provides budget-friendly travel on long-distance routes.
  • Jan Shatabdi Express - A more economical version of the Shatabdi Express, offering both AC and non-AC seating.
  • Sampark Kranti Express - Connects various states and important cities with the national capital, New Delhi.
  • Hirakud Express - One of India's longest-running train services, connecting Odisha with Karnataka over along-distance route.
  • Nandankanan Express - A popular train connecting Bhubaneswar and New Delhi, commonly used by students,professionals, and tourists.
